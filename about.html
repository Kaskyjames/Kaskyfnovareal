About Kasky F. Novareal

I’m a real estate entrepreneur and investor with a passion for building legacies. From a young age, I recognized the power of property to shape lives and communities. That conviction led me to establish Novareal Capital, a company dedicated to sustainable luxury development and smart investments.

Over the years, I’ve been driven by a simple philosophy: real estate should be more than profitable—it should be purposeful. Whether developing landmark projects or mentoring emerging investors, I’m committed to transforming ideas into lasting value.

Outside of my work, I’m a believer in lifelong learning, strategic thinking, and creating pathways for others to thrive.

Building prosperity that outlives us.
